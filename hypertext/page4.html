The First Date

Veronica stood by the door, her hand resting on the handle. Danaya stood next to her, a grin playing on her face.

Danaya: “This is it! You’re really doing it, huh?”

Veronica: “I think so… but there’s one last thing.”

Danaya: “What’s that?”

Veronica glanced at the phone in her hand, then out the window at the lively city streets.

Veronica: “Should I take the taxi or the subway?”

Danaya: “Well, the subway’s quicker, but a taxi’s more convenient—if you’re okay with the cost.”

Veronica sighed, weighing the decision. Will Veronica take the taxi or the subway?
