The First Date

Veronica stared at her phone, Logan’s invitation lingering in her mind. The Nines. Tonight. With me. She turned to Danaya, who was waiting expectantly.

Danaya: “So? What did you say?” Veronica hesitated. She opened her mouth but closed it again.

Veronica: “I can’t. I’m just not ready for this. It feels too… much. I should stay in.”

Danaya looked disappointed but didn’t argue. Later that night, Veronica sat on the couch, a bowl of popcorn in her lap. The familiar opening of The Wedding Singer filled the room, but she barely registered it.

She sighed deeply, her eyes flicking to her phone. Nothing.

She sank deeper into the couch, feeling the weight of her decision. What if I made a mistake? Another sigh escaped her lips as the movie played on, but it offered no comfort. She could’ve been at The Nines… with him. Instead, she was alone, watching a movie she’d seen a hundred times.
