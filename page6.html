The First Date

Veronica took the subway, hoping it would be the faster choice. But as she rushed to the platform, disaster struck—her heel got caught, and with a sharp snap, it broke off.

Veronica: Of course, this would happen to me… Ugh!

She made it to the next station, but now she was stuck. The Nines had a dress code, and there was no way she could show up with a broken heel. She was classier than that! Luckily, there was a thrift store nearby she knew well, and she did have some time to spare.

Should Veronica stop by the thrift store to find a new pair of heels, or should she just walk to The Nines in her broken shoes and take her chances at the fancy lounge?
