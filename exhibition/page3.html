Yearning

Yearning is a powerful emotion, an all-consuming desire for someone that takes over a person. To yearn is to love someone so deeply that nothing else matters. They occupy the only space in your heart. Yearning can be one-sided, where the intensity of love makes it difficult to express those feelings. However, when that yearning is shared, it doesn’t fade—it grows stronger, deepening your love and fueling the longing even more.

To Vincent, with Love (2005) by Jillian McDonald

An interactive website that features the artist, Jillian McDonald, inserting herself into scenes from the film Buffalo 66 alongside actor Vincent Gallo. It explores a one-sided, fictional romance between a fan and a character, highlighting the emotional connection that exists only in the fan’s imagination, with no real-world counterpart.


Notes on Love (2023) by Alica Guo

An interactive website presents a collection of anonymous love letters written by strangers. Users can read these heartfelt notes and are also given the option to write their own love letter, contributing to the ongoing exchange of emotions and connections.
